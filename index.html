Our Specialties

Stone Age Special Cheese

'Cheese' is a famous indica dominant hybrid strain known for its distinctive pungent aroma and physically relaxing effects. It is a robust strain that can be successfully grown outdoors.Indica-dominant hybrid (usually around 60% indica, 40% Sativa).

Ancient Forest Block Berry

The Blockberry strain is a hybrid cross of Black Cherry Punch and Tropicana Cookies. It is known for its high THC content, typically between 28% and 30%, and is recommended for experience users. The buds are dense, olive-green with purple hues and fiery orange hairs, covered in a frosty trichome layer.

Prehistoric Forbiddos

An Indica-dominant hybrid (Forbidden Fruit x Dosidos) that responds well to training techniques like topping and pinching to maximize yields. It has a flowering time of 8-9 weeks (56-63 days) and an outdoor harvest time of late September to early October.
